About Me

Hello! My name is Gopal Vulli, and I am currently a sophomore majoring in Computer Science at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. I have a strong passion for technology and problem-solving, especially in areas such as software development, algorithms, and user interface design.

In my academic journey so far, I’ve completed foundational courses such as CSC 130 (Introduction to Computer Science), CSC 250 (Foundations of Computer Science), and CSC 261 (Computer Organization & Assembly Language). Each course has strengthened my understanding of programming logic, data structures, and the architecture that supports computing systems.

Beyond academics, I enjoy collaborating on group projects where I can combine creativity and technical skills. I believe that computer science isn’t just about coding—it’s about coming together and building a project. My long-term goal is to work as a Software Engineer with a focus on artificial intelligence and systems design.
